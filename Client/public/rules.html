Laser Tag Game Rules

Welcome to the Laser Tag Game! Here are the basic rules to get you started:

General Rules

  1. Players must wear the provided laser tag vest at all times during the game.
  2. Each player is equipped with a laser tag gun and a vest.
  3. The objective is to score points by tagging opponents with your laser gun.
  4. Players must avoid being tagged by opponents to prevent losing points.
  5. The game ends when the time runs out or when a team reaches the target score.

Single Player Mode

  1. Players compete individually to score the highest points.
  2. Each player has a laser tag gun and must tag opponents to score points.
  3. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
  4. No physical contact is allowed or any form of aggressive behavior.

Team Mode

  1. Players are divided into two teams: Red and Blue.
  2. Teams work together to tag opponents and defend their own team members.
  3. The team with the highest combined score at the end of the game wins.
